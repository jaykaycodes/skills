# Style Directions

Use when user asks to push visual expression. Pick one direction and commit.

## Refined Minimal
- Quiet palette, high spacing discipline, sharp copy.
- Emphasis through hierarchy and restraint.

## Editorial
- Typographic contrast and content-forward rhythm.
- Strong sectioning and reading flow.

## Bold Product
- High-contrast accents and assertive action hierarchy.
- Dense but controlled information design.

## Playful Utility
- Friendly tone, expressive accents, clear affordances.
- Delight without sacrificing legibility.

## Direction selection rules
- Match audience context, not personal taste.
- Choose one dominant direction; avoid style mashups.
- Document why the direction supports user goals.
